## Before Publishing

Replace every bracketed placeholder, including company name, mailing address, contact emails, governing law, dispute venue, data vendor list, and effective date. Do not publish these documents unchanged.

These templates do not replace legal advice. They are intended to give counsel and the product team a complete first draft.
