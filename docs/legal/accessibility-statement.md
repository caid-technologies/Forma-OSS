Blueprint is committed to making the Service accessible and usable for as many people as possible.

## 1. Standard

Our goal is to substantially conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA, where practical for the current product stage.

## 2. Ongoing Work

Blueprint is an evolving product. We intend to improve accessibility through design reviews, semantic markup, keyboard navigation, color contrast checks, responsive layouts, alternative text, clear focus states, and testing with assistive technologies.

## 3. Known Limitations

Some generated diagrams, images, 3D scenes, canvas views, or third-party embedded content may not yet provide equivalent accessible alternatives. We will prioritize reasonable fixes when issues are reported.

## 4. Feedback

If you experience an accessibility barrier, contact team@caid-technologies.com with:

- the page or feature where the issue occurred;
- the assistive technology, browser, and device you used, if relevant;
- a short description of the problem;
- your preferred contact method.
